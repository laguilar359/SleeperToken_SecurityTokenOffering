Novus ordo seclorum

Turn your Dead Investments into Profitable Returns.

SLPR Coin is a SECURITY TOKEN OFFERING to raise funds for the investment in to diverse portfolio, which will then be invested for a defined time frame (2 year period). The investors will receive a SLPR token that has potential to appreciate in value At the end of the 2 year period, which they can exchange back into ETH, or they can reinvest the appreciated SLPR token back into the next 2 year SLPR token series. The current investors will receive preference to reinvest into the new token series before any new capital investment from new investors.

SLPR Coin has an investment hurdle rate for the Initial Series 1 STO, and if for any reason the hurdle is not met, the capital raised will be returned to the investors. The initial capital raise hurdle will be $1Million and have an initial cap of $1.5M.

This will allow users that have underperforming or “dead” cryptocurrency coins to exchange into SLPR tokens as well as allowing new capital investment into the SLPR token. After an initial Series 1 offering, the intent is to offer 3-4 offerings a year, each with its own 2 year investment strategy. The token investor will have the option to have any dividends either reinvested back into the strategy or receive the dividends as an income distribution.

There are many regulatory hurdles that currently exist for US investors if they want to get access to the US equity markets directly from their cryptocurrency deposits. Currently, in order to have access to US equity and capital markets, investors must open an account following several suitability, Know Your Client , and Anti Money Laundering regulations .

This new workflow process for US based and approved foreign country investors will incorporate a smart contract that will allow the investor to exchange their cryptocurrency from ETH into SLPR tokens . This would allow the client to get access to the managed portfolio strategies in one or all four markets , and would allow the investor to exit back through the exchange with SLPR tokens that have realized an increase in value. The investor would then be able to exchange the ETH back into their cryptocurrency of their choice at their preferred cryptocurrency exchange.
